- Decision: ITERATE
- Justification: The current VCSF pipeline has demonstrated strong initial performance (Partial AUC 0.1488), confirming that the Wavelet Scattering Transform (WST) and Conditional Normalizing Flow (CNF) architecture is fundamentally correct for this task. However, the identified weaknesses—specifically the reliance on a simplistic Gaussian blur proxy, the potential information loss from aggressive PCA dimensionality reduction, and the instability of gradient-based MLE—are critical "structural robustness" issues. Addressing these will directly improve the "Publishability Delta" by moving the methodology from a heuristic-based approach to a rigorous, physics-informed framework. The ROI of effort is high because the core infrastructure (WST extraction, Zuko flow training) is already functional; the proposed changes (replacing PCA with feature selection, swapping gradient descent for ensemble-based likelihood estimation, and improving the validation proxy) are surgical refinements that significantly increase the likelihood of a top-tier publication and a higher leaderboard rank.
